Our Story

Born from aMother's Love

Native New Yorkers Anna and Eddie Siino started Vincenza's Pizzeria in honor of Anna's mother. What started as a family tradition became Alpharetta's favorite pizza destination — where every slice tells a story and every bite is a journey through flavor.

It's all about good times, great pizza, and living our best cheesy life. Welcome to the party. 🍕
